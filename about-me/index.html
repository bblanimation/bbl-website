About Me

My name is Chris Gearhart – the guy behind the curtain at Bricks Brought to Life – and I thoroughly enjoy creating immersive visuals through stop motion and CG animation.

I've had the pleasure of working on projects for The LEGO Group, Shedd Aquarium in Chicago and Laurie Children's Hospital, along with multiple film projects of my own, which you can find right here on the site!

I discovered this passion through my LEGO collection, and since then I've spent my life following that passion.

All of the artwork here is inspired by, and made for, the greatest designer in existence.
